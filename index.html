About

Justin Harvre

Kait was born in Toronto, Ontario but grew up in Calgary. She took a leap of faith and moved to Mexico for 7 years where she worked in the Airline/Tour Operator business and perfected her second language. Problem solving and negotiation are her passions and there is never a situation she will shy away from. Kait would say she does her best work when she surrounds by a "beautiful chaos of balancing priorities." Kait is fast paced, driven and will start up a conversation with anyone and make you feel like you've known each other for years.
